Research

I am a 5th-year PhD student in Interactive Computing LAB at KAIST, South Korea. I received MS degree in Knowledge Service Engineering from KAIST and BS degree in Computer Science from Gyeongsang National University. My research draws from the fields of Human-Computer Interaction, Crowdsourcing, and Social Computing. I am especially interested in designing and developing crowdsourced policing services with mobile technologies for community safety and public order.
