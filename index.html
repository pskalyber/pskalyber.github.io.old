RESEARCH INTEREST

I am a 5th-year PhD student in Interactive Computing LAB at KAIST, South Korea. My research draws from the fields of Human-Computer Interaction, Crowdsourcing, and Social Computing. I am especially interested in designing and evaluating crowdsourced policing services with mobile technologies for community safety and public order.
